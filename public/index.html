Feet Up

Helping others get their feet up off the ground.

About

"Give a man a fish...

...you feed him for a day. But teach a man to fish - you feed him for a lifetime "


Feet Up looks to provide those who are less fortunate with the opportunities to succeed. Our service aims to expose the talents of those living on the streets to people like you. Through your donations, you can help provide homeless men and women the resources for them to pursue their passions, better themselves, with the hopes of leading a productive life.

Our Stories

Story #1: Jane Doe

Jane has been painting for the past 10 years. Although she can barely scrape enough money for supplies, she has managed to produce an occasional painting, which nets her some money. Through Feet Up, she has been able to pursue her artistic passions more and more. Click to view her story here.

Story #2: John Doe

John has been an active guitar player all of his life. However, when he was evicted from his home, he lost everything, including his guitar. Since he was introduced back to Feet Up, he has since been able to buy a guitar and has been practicing in hopes to play in a band. More on his story here.

Story #3: Janet John

Janet John was introduced to Feet Up just recently, but she has already seen improvement. She notices that several people continue to stop and say hello to her daily. She appreciates the support, as people complement her on her knitting. She's even had the chance to interview for a job. You can read up more on her story at our site
